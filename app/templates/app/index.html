RightSize estimates sample sizes for your studies. To begin, first determine the type of endpoint for your study by clicking on one of the buttons below.

Means/Quantities

Choose this if your endpoint is numerical, meaning you are measuring amounts for each subject and comparing the amounts between groups (e.g. tumor size, cytokine expression, or body weight).

Rates/Proportions

Choose this if your endpoint is categorical, meaning you are measuring whether or not an endpoint has been achieved in each subject and comparing the proportions between groups (e.g. the occurance of adverse events, metastasis, or death).

Survival

Choose this if your endpoint is time to event, meaning you are measuring the time to a particular event (e.g. time to death, or time to metastasis).
